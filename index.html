Privacy Policy

HomeLedger is a fully offline app. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter (such as budgets, bills, reminders, net worth, and household info) is stored locally on your device.

  • No account required
  • No analytics
  • No tracking
  • No external servers
  • No selling or sharing data

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed from your device (subject to your device backup settings).

Notifications

Notifications are optional and controlled by you. They are scheduled and generated locally on your device and are not sent to any server.

Subscriptions

HomeLedger Pro is offered as an auto-renewing subscription through the Apple App Store. HomeLedger does not have access to your payment information.

Pricing
  • Monthly: $4.99 per month
  • Annual: $49.99 per year

Eligible users may receive a 7-day free trial on monthly or annual plans as an Apple introductory offer. Trial eligibility is determined by Apple.

Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at the confirmation of purchase (or after any applicable free trial). Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple ID account settings in the App Store.
