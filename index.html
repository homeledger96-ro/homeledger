Privacy Policy

HomeLedger is a fully offline app.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All data you enter (budgets, bills, reminders, net worth, household info) is stored locally on your device only.

  • Does not use analytics
  • Does not track users
  • Does not require an account
  • Does not connect to external servers
  • Does not sell or share data

If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device.

Notifications

Notifications are optional and controlled by you. They are generated locally on your device and never sent to any server.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are handled securely by Apple via the App Store. HomeLedger does not have access to your payment information.
